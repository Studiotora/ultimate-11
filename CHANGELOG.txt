[game.js] DELTA-TIME MOVEMENT FIX
  - Animation loop now passes dt (delta time scalar) to tick(), moveOffBall(), tickBallTravel()
  - dt = elapsed ms / 16.667 (target 60fps frame), capped at 3
  - On 30fps mobile: dt≈2, step doubles per frame → same real-world speed as 60fps PC
  - Carrier movement: mv.x/y multiplied by dt, clamp scaled by dt
  - Defender steps (engager + cover): MAX_DEF_STEP * pressMult * dt
  - Off-ball speeds (DRIFT/ROLE/SUPPORT/STRIKER): all scaled by dt
  - Ball travel progress: += dt instead of ++
  - Ball lerp: Math.min(.18*dt, 1)
  - Stamina drain/regen: scaled by dt
  - Result: movement speed now frame-rate independent across all devices
